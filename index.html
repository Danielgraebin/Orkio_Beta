The problem

Organizations want AI to run real workflows — but today’s stack is fragmented: assistants in one place, automations in another, documents elsewhere. This creates risk, lack of traceability, and uncontrolled execution.

  • Low auditability: hard to justify decisions and actions.
  • Security gaps: sensitive data spread across tools.
  • Operational friction: workflows remain manual and slow.
  • Limited governance: no clear policies, approvals, or trails.

The solution

Orkio adds an executive AI layer on top of operations: it decomposes goals into tasks, assigns specialist agents, integrates tools, executes workflows, and records everything with governance.

  • Orchestrator: planning, delegation, approvals.
  • Specialist agents: research, knowledge, engineering, operations.
  • Connectors: systems, data sources, and communication channels.
  • Trust & Audit: policies, logs, traceability.

How it works (Multi-Agent Workflow)

Explained

Orkio follows a simple operational model: a user defines a mission, the Orchestrator breaks it into tasks, delegates to specialist agents, consolidates results, and keeps an auditable record.

Workflow overview: Mission → Task Queue → Specialist Agents → Results Store → Monitoring & Audit.
