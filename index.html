The problem

Companies want AI to execute real workflows, but most tools are fragmented and not governable: chat here, automation there — with limited traceability and control.

  • Low auditability and higher operational risk
  • Integrations scattered and hard to maintain
  • No tenant isolation for multiple organizations (multi-tenant)
  • Unclear access policies, retention rules, and audit trails

What Orkio is

Orkio is an executive AI that coordinates agents and tools like a real organization: you define the objective, and Orkio distributes work, integrates systems, executes tasks, and logs everything — with policies and auditability.

  • Chat (threads, files, actions)
  • Studio (agent builder and configuration)
  • Agents Graph (handoff, notify, invoke)
  • Connectors Hub + Trust & Audit

Why Orkio stands out

B2B

Centralized execution core

Instead of dozens of disconnected AIs, Orkio builds a central decision core that coordinates agents, data, and business systems.

Agents as executives

Each agent has a clear role: CEO AI, CFO AI, Operations, Data, Compliance — collaborating with handoffs and responsibilities.

Governance by design

Logs, policies, tenant isolation, and auditability are not add-ons. They are part of the architecture from day one — ideal for critical operations.

End-to-end audit trails

Every action executed by agents can be tracked, explained, and exported. Sensitive actions can require approvals.

  • RBAC/ABAC and tenant isolation
  • Exportable trails (CSV/JSON) for audit and compliance
  • Hierarchical control for high-risk actions
