The problem

Companies want AI to run real workflows — but today’s stack is fragmented: chat assistants here, automations there, files somewhere else. This creates risk, poor traceability, and uncontrolled execution.

  • Low auditability: hard to justify decisions and actions.
  • Security gaps: sensitive data spread across tools.
  • Operational friction: workflows remain manual and slow.
  • Limited governance: no clear approvals, policies, or trails.

The solution

Orkio adds an executive AI layer on top of operations: it decomposes goals into tasks, assigns specialist agents, integrates tools, executes workflows, and records everything with governance.

Orchestrator Plans, delegates, monitors progress, and consolidates results.
Specialist Agents Research, knowledge, engineering, compliance, operations.
Trust & Audit Policies, logs, and traceability by design.
